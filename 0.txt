Chicken Breast
1 Serving

Chicken Breast, 1 breast
Salt, 1 pinch
Pepper, 1 pinch

1. Dethaw chicken breast
2. Season chicken breast with salt and pepper
3. Put breast on a hot skillet on one side for 10 minutes
4. Flip breast and cook for an additional 8 minutes
5. Remove from heat and allow to cool before serving
