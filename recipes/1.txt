Spaghetti
4 servings

Spaghetti Noodles, 12 oz
Spaghetti Sauce, 1 jar
Salt, 1 pinch

1. Heat 5 cups of water in a pot until boiling
2. Add salt
3. Place noodles in boiling water
4. Allow to cook for 12 minutes, then remove from heat
5. Empty jar of spaghetti sauce into a smaller pot and heat until beginning to bubble, stirring often
6. Remove sauce from heat, and allow to cook for 2 minutes
7. Combine on plate and enjoy
