Fried Rice
1 Serving

Rice, 1 cup
Soy Sauce, 3 splashes
Assorted Vegtables, 0.5 cup
Butter, 1 tsp

1. Cook rice as normal until fluffy, or take 1 cup of day old rice
2. Add butter into a pan and melt
3. Once butter is melted, add rice and cook until rice begins to brown. Stir/flip often.
4. Add vegtables and cook until desired tenderness
5. Add soy sauce and cook for 30 seconds more
6. Remove from heat and allow to cool
7. Enjoy
